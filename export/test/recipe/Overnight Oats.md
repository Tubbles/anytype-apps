# Overnight Oats

## Ingredients   
- 1/2 cup rolled oats   
- 1/2 cup milk   
- 1/4 cup yogurt   
- 1 tbsp chia seeds   
- 1 tbsp honey   
- Fresh fruit for topping   
   
## Instructions   
1. Mix oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, and honey in a jar   
2. Refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours)   
3. Top with fresh fruit before serving

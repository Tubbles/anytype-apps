# Chicken Stir-Fry

## Ingredients   
- 500g chicken breast, sliced   
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)   
- 3 tbsp soy sauce   
- 1 tbsp sesame oil   
- 2 cloves garlic, minced   
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated   
- Rice for serving   
   
## Instructions   
1. Cook rice according to package directions   
2. Heat sesame oil in a wok over high heat   
3. Cook chicken until golden, about 5 minutes   
4. Add garlic, ginger, and vegetables, stir-fry 3-4 minutes   
5. Add soy sauce, toss to combine   
6. Serve over rice
